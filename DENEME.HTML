Our Story

The idea for our team emerged from countless late-night brainstorming sessions during our senior year. Burcu, Betül, and Sude had previously worked together on various university projects and realized they shared a common vision: using technology to solve real-world problems.

Motivated by our mutual interests and fueled by the challenge Teknofest offers, we came together with a strong sense of purpose and friendship. Our journey is not only about building a solution— it's about building something together.
